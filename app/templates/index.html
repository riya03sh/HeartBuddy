Why Heart Health Matters

Heart disease is the leading cause of death globally, responsible for 17.9 million deaths each year. Early detection and lifestyle changes can prevent 80% of premature heart disease.

  • Eat a heart-healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly (150 mins/week)
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Manage stress effectively
  • Get regular health screenings

Regular risk assessments can identify potential issues before symptoms appear. Our tool helps you understand your risk factors and take proactive steps toward better heart health.
